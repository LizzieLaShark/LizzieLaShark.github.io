Daily Mindfulness

Lizzie 1/2/16

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I just did the informal one. I did it when my friend was telling me about some dramatic stuff that's going on in her love life. She seemed really grateful for a listening ear. When I repeated back to her what I had heard I think she got a bit confused because it's a little out of character, normally that is the sort of behaviour she would observe in me only when I'm facilitating a group. Nevertheless I think she felt really satisfied with the conversation.


Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I'm going to try the 12 minute focused attention and open attention technique


Any other musings?

I was interested in his metaphors around experience level. I suppose it made me think about how even though I've been meditating for quite some time, because my practise isnt regular, I'm still at the beginner stage. I basically only meditate when I'm stressed out. I'm trying to use it as more of a preventative thing these days. Watch this space...
